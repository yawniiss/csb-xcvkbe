Built on the zkSync network, zkGhost is a community-owned fair launch token. The project initiative seeks to familiarize individuals with the zkSync ecosystem using ghosts that represent people feeling lonely in their lives.

The goal of zkGhost would be to provide a community for individuals who feel isolated or disconnected, where they can connect with others who share similar interests and experiences. The token would be used to access exclusive content, participate in community events, and receive discounts on products and services related to the theme of the community.

Overall, zkGhost would serve as a platform for individuals to connect with others, access exclusive benefits, and support charitable causes related to loneliness and isolation. The token would provide a means of access to these benefits and serve as a way to support the community's mission

Wait, you mean that if ETH pumps, Ghost pumps with it?

Yes! Let’s take an example: If ETH increases in price, it won’t ‘outrun’ Ghost. The APR will vary in terms of its USD value, but emissions won’t. This is something that wouldn’t be possible with another 1:1 pegged asset like a stable coin LP position, where the USD value is directly tied to the emissions. If ETH rises in USD value, Ghost goes with it. Same if ETH falls in USD value, Ghost will be worth less in USD, but it won’t affect the peg. Peg is solely determined based on buying and selling volume of Ghost.
